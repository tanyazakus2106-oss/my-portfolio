<objective>
Refine the existing `<Image>` `widths` arrays on the two high-impact image surfaces called out in CONTEXT.md D-06: the About hero photo and the case study cover image. Both already have `widths` + `sizes` props; the refinement adds the missing DPR-2 mobile steps so 375 px iPhones (DPR 2 or 3) fetch an appropriately sized asset instead of the largest variant.

Per RESEARCH section 2 Surface 1: About hero is portrait `aspect-[4/5]` in a ~520-540 px constrained half-column at lg+ and full-column-width minus padding below lg. Current widths `[560, 1120]` jumps directly from desktop column to 2x desktop column, skipping mobile.

Per RESEARCH section 2 Surface 2: Case study cover spans 1152 px at desktop (max-w-[1200px] minus 48 px padding) and 100vw minus 3rem below 1200 px. Current widths `[900, 1800]` jumps from 900 to 2 x desktop, skipping mobile entirely.

This plan is two surgical `widths` array edits. Neither `sizes` formula changes — both are research-verified correct.

Implements: RESP-02 (responsive images load appropriately sized assets per viewport), CASE-06 (case study mobile readability — cover image must not be 1800 px on 375 px mobile).
</objective>
