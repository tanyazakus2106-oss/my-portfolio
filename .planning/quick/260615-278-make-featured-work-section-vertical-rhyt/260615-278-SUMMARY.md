# Quick Task 260615-278 — Summary

## What changed

Made the home page featured-work ("Projects") section's vertical spacing fluid,
scaling with viewport width instead of snapping at the 768px (`md`) breakpoint.

**`src/styles/global.css`** — added a shared `@theme` token:

```css
--spacing-section: clamp(64px, 40px + 6.39vw, 132px);
```

**`src/pages/index.astro`** — replaced three stepped utilities with the token:

| Gap | Before | After |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Divider → heading (section `pt`) | `pt-[var(--spacing-3xl)] md:pt-[132px]` | `pt-[var(--spacing-section)]` |
| Last card → footer (section `pb`) | `pb-[var(--spacing-3xl)] md:pb-[132px]` | `pb-[var(--spacing-section)]` |
| Between cards (flex `gap`) | `gap-[var(--spacing-3xl)] md:gap-[132px]` | `gap-[var(--spacing-section)]` |

## Result

All three gaps now scale continuously from 64px (mobile) to 132px at a 1440px
viewport, capping at 132px on wider screens. No breakpoint snap. Consistent with
the horizontal-gutter clamp from quick task 260615-1js.
