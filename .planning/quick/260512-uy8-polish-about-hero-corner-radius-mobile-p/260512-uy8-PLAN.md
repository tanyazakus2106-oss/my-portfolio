## Task 3 — Suppress focus state on touch devices

**File:** `src/styles/global.css`
**Change:** Append a `@media (hover: none) and (pointer: coarse)` block that strips `outline` from `:focus` and `:focus-visible` site-wide. This targets phones/tablets without an attached pointing device. Keyboard navigation on desktop and tablets with a hardware keyboard + mouse remains unchanged.

**Rationale:** Site already uses `:focus-visible` (keyboard-only) outlines, but iOS Safari has a known quirk where `:focus-visible` matches after a tap on `<a>`/`<button>` elements, leaving a stray accent outline after touch. The standard remedy is the `(hover: none) and (pointer: coarse)` media query.

**Verify:** On a touch device (or browser devtools mobile emulation), tapping any link or button no longer leaves an accent outline. On desktop, keyboard `Tab` still shows the accent outline.
